Weddings

“Once in a while, right in the middle of an ordinary life, love gives us a fairytale”

That fascinating moment where your hearts got interlocked , future paths intertwined carves another milestone along your memory lane.
That mystical split second engraved deep within your heart would be sculpted into a mesmerizing glorious celebration with our assistance provided. Irrespective of the scale, complexity of your wedding an unparalleled service would be delivered to the entire client base.
Devent- the pinnacle of the event management industry undertakes a three part philosophy: intricate planning, seamless execution and topnotch professionalism in rendering a superior quality customer centric service. So under the immediate constant supervision of Devent your wedding would nevertheless be an enthralling experience executed without a hitch.
Amalgamating the novel trends in the industry your wedding, the inception to a new chapter will be smoothly orchestrated to echo the very sentiments of the couple. Handover your overwhelming burden to us and enjoy to the fullest capacity on your big day!

Our cluster of services comprises of:

  • Initial discussion on the details over a coffee, involving the bride and groom

  • Preparation of an appropriate time frame and scheduling of work upon the initial consultation

  • Preparation of a customer friendly budget based on your financial specifications

  • Handpicking of the idyllic venues by our event planning crew

  • Joint site tours prior to the location arrangements and finalizing

  • Handling the food and catering arrangements comprising of mouth watering delicacies (wedding cake inclusive)

  • Offer guidance and advisory support on the relevant theme to be adapted and the blending colour elements to be integrated to the event internal setting

  • Arrangement of a live performing band and sound/ audio system coordination to tinge your wondrous moment with life & bliss

  • Internal setting and decoration arrangements to inject colour to the fond memories that would last a lifetime

  • Agenda preparation and On site coordination to ensure the seamless execution without a glitch

  • Connecting the client with the professional vendors in sorting out the required logistics (Invitation printing and coordination, florists, wedding car rentals, lighting specialists, Attire for groom, bride and retinue and ceremony conductors)

  • Contracting photographers and videographers to capture the intriguing chapter in your lives

  • Arrangement of dancers if required by the client to create a soothing atmosphere for the attendees to relish within
  • Final tour with the client after all required arrangements and coordination is done
  • Arrangement and conducting of a complete rehearsal based on the special requests of the client
